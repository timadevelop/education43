My name is Vlad. I've got my software engineering degree in Sofia University, Bulgaria. You can find more info about me here.

I was blessed enough to be able to study for free. Unfortunately, I've seen a lot of people struggle due to their student loans. It's disgusting.

We do not need to blame anyone. It's not about goverement, money, or specific people in education industry. Creating this simple webpage I want to show that traditional education is not mandatory. Instead of blaming we can find alternative ways to get your knowledge.

While studying in Sofia University, I did not attend lectures often. I discovered that self-education is more effective than sitting in university and watching how someone reads a presentation for you. Remember, I do not blame anyone. FMI @Sofia is an amazing place with smart and cool people, but it's not the same everywhere.

I hope these resources will help you realize that having access to the Internet you have access to any knowledge for free.

I believe that self-education is much more effective than our education system. Stop thinking that some piece of paper is more important than your actual abilities. Everything in the market is based on a simple supply-and-demand theory, there's a chance to stop those crazy student loans.
